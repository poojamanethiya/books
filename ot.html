THE TAMING OF THE SHREW
Lucentio loves Bianca but cannot court her until her shrewish older sister
Katherina marries. The eccentric Petruccio marries the reluctant Katherina and uses a
number of tactics to render her an obedient wife. Lucentio marries Bianca and,
in a contest at the end, Katherina proves to be the most obedient wife.

REVIEW: This story revolves around katherine who is shrewish and seems to hate man, this story is somewhat funny as petruccio tries to make katherina an obedient wife , but in mostly parts of his trying he just do embarrassing acts or act wierdly , overall the story is entertaining.

AUTHOR: William Shakespeare
